randRange( 10, 40) U*(U+1)/2

Evaluate \displaystyle\sum\limits_{i=1}^{U} i

ANSWER

The sum formula says \displaystyle\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n} i = \dfrac{n \cdot (n+1)}{2}

So \displaystyle\sum\limits_{i=1}^{U} i =\dfrac{U \cdot U+1}{2} = U*(U+1)/2

ANSWER

So \displaystyle\sum\limits_{i=1}^{U} i^2 =\dfrac{U \cdot U+1 \cdot 2*U+1}{6} = U*(U+1)*(2*U+1)/6
